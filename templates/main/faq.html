Frequently Asked Questions

Posting an ad on our platform is quick and easy. Simply create an account, navigate to the "Post Ad" section, and provide details about your item, including category, description, price, and images. Make sure to provide accurate and compelling information to attract potential buyers.

At present, posting ads on our platform is free. You can list your items and connect with potential buyers without incurring any charges. However, please review our terms and conditions for any updates regarding fees or premium ad services in the future.

Your ad will remain visible on the platform for a specified duration, typically 90 days. After that period, you can choose to renew or repost your ad if needed. However, please note that ads violating our terms and policies may be removed earlier.

If you have questions or inquiries about a product, you can contact the seller directly through the product listing page. Look for the "Contact Seller" button or a provided contact form. Fill out the necessary information and compose your message. The seller will receive your inquiry and respond to you accordingly.

To search for products on Anymart, you can use the search bar located at the top of every page. Enter relevant keywords or specific product names and press Enter. The search results page will display matching products based on your search query.

To list your products on Anymart, log in to your account and navigate to the "Seller Dashboard." Click on the "Add Product" button, and provide details about your product, including title, description, category, price, and images. Submit the form, and your product will be listed on the marketplace.

While we provide a platform for buyers and sellers to connect, it is important to prioritize your personal safety. When meeting with a seller or buyer, we recommend choosing a public location and taking necessary precautions. It's advisable to inspect the item thoroughly and, if feasible, bring a friend or family member along.

If you encounter any issues with your order, such as receiving a damaged item or not receiving the item at all, please contact the seller directly as soon as possible. They will assist you in resolving the problem. If needed, Anymart's customer support team is available to help mediate the situation.

We take the security of our users seriously. If you encounter any suspicious or fraudulent activity, we encourage you to report it immediately. Please use the "Report Ad" feature or contact our support team, providing as much information as possible. We will investigate the matter promptly.
