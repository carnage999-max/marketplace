Privacy Policy

Information Collection

Anymart collects personal information from users when they register an account, create a profile, or engage in transactions on our platform. This information may include name, email address, contact details, and other relevant details needed to provide our services.

Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate user account creation and management
  • Enable communication between buyers and sellers
  • Process transactions and provide customer support
  • Analyze user behavior and improve our services
  • Send relevant updates and promotional messages (with user consent)

Data Security

Anymart takes appropriate security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We implement industry-standard practices, including encryption and secure protocols, to safeguard user information.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services, such as payment gateways or analytics providers, to enhance our platform's functionality and user experience. These services may have their own privacy policies, and users are encouraged to review them for more information about how their data is handled.

Cookie Policy

Anymart uses cookies to enhance user experience, track user sessions, and gather usage statistics. By using our platform, users consent to the use of cookies as outlined in our Cookie Policy.

Data Retention

We retain user data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Users can request the deletion of their account and associated data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Anymart does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child without parental consent, we take steps to remove the information from our servers.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update or modify this privacy policy from time to time. It is your responsibility to review the policy periodically for any changes. Continued use of Anymart after the changes indicates your acceptance of the modified privacy policy.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@anymart.com.
